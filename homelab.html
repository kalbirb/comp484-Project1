Now that we have at least understood this hobby and are invested into expanding our IT skills, we can start thinking about scalability to our lab.

Networking

Right now, you may be plugging in your server into your home network through one of the ports of your wifi router. This is great for beginners but we want to dive in deep into our homelab experience. You can invest in network switches from brands such as Cisco, Netgear, and Unifi. These switches provide expandability to allow us to plug in other devices such as a Raspberry Pis, smart home IOT, and additional servers.

Expanding Space

Now that we have a networking backplane, you might start running out of space on your desk. Investing in a server rack is a good start to consolidating and visually making your homelab look better for those sweet pics. Pictured on the right is a mini server rack that can be used to house the small form factor PCs such as a mini PC, network switches, or single board computers like raspberry Pis.

At some point during your homelab journey, you might become interested in using your homelab to host services for your friends or use it in a professional setting.

Homelab in a Professional setting

When you start reaching toward this phase in homelabbing, you need to consider what qualities an enterprise system requires. Terms such as Service Level Agreement, which dictates the uptime your service is required to have contractually, Quality of Service, and Security comes into play. IF you decide to go down this route, you should take it slow and plan out and test every step. Start simple and try out enterprise systems like running and managing a firewall. Test out hosting a simple game server for your friends and family to play on and see how that impacts your homelab's performance.
